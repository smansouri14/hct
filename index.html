Our History

Husainiya Center of Tucson (HCT) is a non-profit Organization that was approved by the IRS. The Organization is also a tax-exempt non-profit religious foundation under the IRS.

The HCT is a religious and cultural Islamic Shia’a center based on the teachings of our beloved Prophet Mohammad (PBUH) and his Progeny, 12 Imams (AS). The purpose for which this HCT is established is based on the belief that Islam is a true way of life. Based on this principle, the HCT’s Mission is to practice Islam as a way of life, to promote Islamic ideas, teaching, and cultures in Tucson, Arizona in particular, and throughout the State of Arizona as well as the USA and beyond in general.

The HCT devoted volunteer teachers teach the children the true Islamic thoughts and cultures and especially clarify to them to be aware of the false and baseless Islamic ideas that are, unfortunately, spreading among the communities everywhere and should not be trapped with these falsehoods. Another major activity that the HCT has is helping the refugees and the new comers for finding jobs and to be established and blend in the community. In addition to the establishment of the Islamic studies, the HCT’s further Mission is to promote friendly relations and understanding among Muslim communities and the followers of the other religions, the operation to be carried mainly within the State of Arizona, specifically in Tucson, Arizona.

HCT was operating in various houses on temporary basis for many years until finally by the Grace of Almighty Allah (SWT) this new center was established and officially opened on April 21, 2019, 15th of Sha’aban, the Anniversary of the birth of our beloved 12th Imam, Imam Mahdi A’ajalallah Ta’ala Farajah (May Allah fasten his coming).
